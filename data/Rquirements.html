04. Child passengers older than 7 years but less than 13 years
  • Provided the procedure for unaccompanied, 7 - 13 years old of children is followed can fly unaccompanied.
  • They are not allowed to travel on connection flights.
Guests who are over 13 but under 18 may travel alone without applying the procedure for Unaccompanied Children. They may not, however, travel accompanied by children under the age of 13.
